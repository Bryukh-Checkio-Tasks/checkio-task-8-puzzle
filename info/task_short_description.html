8 puzzle is a sliding puzzle that consists of a frame of randomly ordered, numbered square tiles with one missing tile. The puzzle is presented as a 3x3 matrix with numbers from 1 to 8. Zero is the empty cell. You can "move" the empty cell in four directions: up--"U", down--"D", left--"L", and right--"R". You need to return a sequence of moves to solve the puzzle. The solution is presented as string of symbols ("UDLR") describing your moves.
